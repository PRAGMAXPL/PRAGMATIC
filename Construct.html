How It Works

From minting your first Construct to trading on the open market, here’s everything you need to know.

The Minting Flow

1

Connect Wallet

Use the "Connect" button to link your MetaMask wallet to the Plasma network.

2

Approve Tokens

A one-time approval for 200,000 `$PRAGMA` tokens is required to enable the minting contract.

3

Spawn Construct

Call the `spawnPRAGMA()` function. This burns your dust and mints one of the 666 exclusive NFTs.

4

Fragment & Trade

Your new NFT automatically fragments into ~10k `$PRAGMA` shards, making it instantly tradable.

Trading & Auditing

Trading on Uniswap

The primary liquidity pool consists of `$PRAGMA` and Construct shards. Swap tokens to acquire partial NFT ownership, or sell a full NFT for its equivalent token value.

Viewing & Auditing

Use the in-app modals to watch animated visualizations of the protocol, explore the rarity of different traits, and run a local audit to verify the on-chain distribution.

Get Started

Ready to join the ecosystem? Follow these simple steps to set up your wallet and spawn your first PRAGMA Construct.

1. Wallet Setup

  • Install MetaMask or a compatible wallet.
  • Add the Plasma Network with these details:
    • RPC URL: `https://rpc.plasma.to`
    • Chain ID: `9745`
    • Symbol: `PLASMA`
  • Get test `$PRAGMA` from the official faucet.

2. Spawn Your Construct

  • Connect your newly configured wallet on the main site.
  • Approve the contract to use 200,000 of your `$PRAGMA`.
  • Click the "Spawn PRAGMA" button.
  • View your unique NFT in the post-mint modal!
